Welcome to Utah Lake Parameters App!

The Ambient Water Quality Monitoring System (AWQMS) is a web-based data management system for ambient water quality data from Utah State. This app displays in a map the AWQMS data from Utah Lake stations for a selected parameters.

Click the map to get information about the station.
